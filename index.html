Complete Photo Verification System

Checkmark provides a complete ecosystem for capturing, sharing, and verifying authentic photos.

Camera Tab

Capture photos with real-time verification. Toggle "Public" to generate a shareable verification code. Without it, photos get a checkmark watermark only‚Äîno code, completely private.

Public Photos

When you enable "Public" before capturing, the photo receives both a checkmark watermark AND a unique verification code (e.g., CHK-A7F3). This code appears on the photo and can be shared with anyone.

Verify Tab

Anyone with a verification code can view the original, unedited photo stored on Checkmark's servers. Enter the code to see the authentic image, timestamp, device info, and cryptographic proof of authenticity.

How Verification Works

We don't use AI detection. Instead, we verify photos at the moment of capture using device signatures, hardware attestation, timestamps, and cryptographic signing. This creates mathematical proof that's impossible to fake.

How It Works

Checkmark uses cryptographic proof, not AI detection, to verify photo authenticity

1

Device Authentication

Verifies your device's unique hardware signature using Secure Enclave or TEE to confirm the photo comes from a real, uncompromised camera.

2

Capture Verification

Records precise timestamp, GPS coordinates, camera settings, and device information at the moment of capture with cryptographic signing.

3

Anti-Spoofing

Checks hardware attestation certificates to ensure the photo came from an authentic camera sensor, not a virtual camera or screen recording.

4

Cryptographic Proof

The photo and metadata are signed using public-key cryptography, creating an unforgeable mathematical proof.

5

Blockchain Recording

Verification signature is recorded on a public blockchain, creating an immutable, timestamped record.

6

Public Verification

If made public, anyone can enter the code to view the original unedited photo and confirm authenticity.
